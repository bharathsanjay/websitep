Banu Pattu Pai centre is a start-up initiated under SHRI DST project titled Technological Interventions for Indigenous Handcrafted Products of Tamilnadu' which aims to promote and sustain the traditional handwoven silk mat weaving industry of Pattomadai. Mrs. Haiwa Beevi, the fine silk mat weaver from Pattamadai, Tirunelveli is the master weaver of this Banu Pattu Pai Centre. She along with her daughter Banu and a Team of skilled weavers is running this centre successfully with a support of designers from SHRI team.

The design team provides guidance in terms of colour combinations and new design patterns which are crafted into an wonderful masterpiece by the talented weavers with utmost care without compromising its quality and fineness. This team work resulted in various new designer mats such as Naanal, Kolam etc. which are of high demand among the customers. Another speciality of Banu pattu pai centre is that the mats can be customised according to the customers need, type of dye and colour preference.
